Watched Categories

How to:

Add a category

Click on the "Add a category" button, and search for a game. After clicking the game you can see all its categories and add the ones you like.

Go to a speedrun.com leaderboard

Click on a category to open the full leaderboard on speedrun.com in a new tab.

Remove a watched category

Right click on the category, and then click the "Remove" button.

Reorder categories

Click and drag on a category to move it to a new position.

Reorder games

Click on the arrows next to a game title to move the game up and down in the list.
